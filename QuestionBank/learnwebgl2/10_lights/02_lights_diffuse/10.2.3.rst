  The dot product of two vectors is calculated using which of the following formulas?

  - :code:`v0[0]*v1[0]  +  v0[1]*v1[1]  +  v0[2]*v1[2]`

    + Correct. Multiply the corresponding terms and add.

  - :code:`v0[0]+v1[0]  *  v0[1]+v1[1]  *  v0[2]+v1[2]`

    - Incorrect.

  - :code:`v0[0]*v1[0]  *  v0[1]*v1[1]  *  v0[2]*v1[2]`

    - Incorrect.

  - :code:`v0[0] - v1[0]  +  v0[1] - v1[1]  +  v0[2] - v1[2]`

    - Incorrect.
