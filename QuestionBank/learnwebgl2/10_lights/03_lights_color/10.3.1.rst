  A flashlight that shines a pure blue light would have a color of?

  - :code:`(0.0, 0.0, 1.0)`

    + Correct.

  - :code:`(1.0, 0.0, 0.0)`

    - Incorrect. This is a red light.

  - :code:`(1.0, 1.0, 1.0)`

    - Incorrect. This is a white light.

  - :code:`(1.0, 1.0, 0.0)`

    - Incorrect. This is a yellow light.
