   You are asked to develop a cash register for a BOB´S DONUTS shop (Minervan´s favourite place in SF) that sells chocolate donuts and vanilla donuts. At first it will ask the name of the customer and then the number of chocolate and vanilla donuts they would like to buy. And then print a summary of what they bought along with the bill as illustrated in the session below:
   ::


      Name of Customer?
      Harry
      Chocolate Donuts are $1.50 each. How many Chocolate Donuts?
      1
      Vanilla Donuts are $1.25 each. How many Vanilla Donuts?
      2
      Harry, you bought 1 Chocolate Donut(s) and 2 Vanilla Donut(s).
      Your bill is $ 4.0
