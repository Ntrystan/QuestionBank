    Which of the following pairs of statements has two statements that are NOT    
    equivalent?    
    

    - ``&arr[0];`` and ``arr;``    
      

      - *(arr + sizeof) != *(arr) + sizeof
    - ``arr[0];`` and ``*arr;``    
      

      - *(arr + sizeof) != *(arr) + sizeof
    - ``*(arr + 1);`` and ``arr[1];``    
      

      - *(arr + sizeof) != *(arr) + sizeof
    - ``*(arr + 1);`` and ``*(arr) + sizeof(arr[0]);``    
      

      + Correct!
